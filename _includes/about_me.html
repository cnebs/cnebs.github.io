Bio

Hi, there. I'm a developer currently working as a Staff Engineer for IBM Systems Group. While I studied Neuroscience in college and initially intended on attending medical school, my university experience building a PHP & Drupal e-commerce site for a startup brought me to a fork in the road upon graduation. I decided to take a leap of faith to follow my new-found passion by teaching myself Node, Typescript, some modern frameworks, and attending Hack Reactor in 2018. Just a few months after starting that path, I started my first software job.

I enjoy learning new design patterns, contributing to architectural designs, writing user-driven code, and practicing algorithms. I have proficiency in the Node and Python ecosystems as well as experience building scalable services using both microservices and event-driven architectures. I've also designed and produced scalable web-applications for clients; if you have application or site build needs, feel free to fill out the contact form below.

Outside of the office, I enjoy traveling, hiking, powerlifting, videogames, sci-fi novels, and working with bare-metal cloud on my raspberry pis.

Bio

Hi, there! I'm Charles, a full-stack software engineer based in Austin, TX. I'm endlessly curious about all things tech. My specialty is the web, and I've spent the last few years working with a range of technologies including React, Node, Express, and Python to build event-driven microservices. I've been coding since 2011, where I was first exposed to an IDE while writing mods for Minecraft servers. Since then, I've explored a lot of areas including investment automation with Python, e-commerce with Drupal, and even tinkering with Raspberry Pis in my own home lab. My passion is in building (and at times designing) exceptional, accessible, test-driven, and human-centered interfaces that are a pleasure to use.

Professionally, I've had the opportunity to lead a front-end rewrite of a sales gamification platform. I've also had the chance to build proof of concepts for enterprise clients, helping them to visualize and understand the potential of enterprise technologies. In addition, I've co-authored an IBM development course and exam to share knowledge and skills with my colleagues. The bulk of my professional experience has been spent developing the UI of an enterprise support platform, and along the way, I've been fortunate enough to guide and mentor interns and junior engineers.

In my free time, when I'm not learning new tech, I like traveling, hiking, powerlifting, reading, and competitive gaming.
